I am **Tushar Choudhury** — an Identity and Access Management (IAM) professional, birdwatcher, and someone who thinks deeply about how cities are built and could be built differently.

---

## What I Do

My primary work is in **Identity and Access Management**. I help organisations design and implement secure, scalable systems that control who gets access to what — and when. The IAM space sits at the intersection of security, user experience, and enterprise architecture, which makes it endlessly interesting.

---

## Why This Blog

I believe that knowledge shared openly is knowledge that compounds. This blog is my small contribution to that:

- **For IAM professionals:** Practical guides, architecture patterns, and opinions on where the industry is heading.
- **For birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts:** Field notes, photography, and thoughts on conservation — particularly from the Indian subcontinent, which is one of the most biodiverse regions on Earth.
- **For urban thinkers:** Ideas on sustainable city planning, transit, and how Indian cities can chart a better path.
